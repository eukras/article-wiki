Simple Tables

There are better ways to enter table data, but this is sometimes sufficient and extremely simple. See @[formatting-tables] (below).

DEMO ---
! Name ! Number
| John | 7326532765
| Jane | 6397532340 ksjdh kjsh kdsjh sdkjh dskjh dskjh 

= Table $[t++]. ** A Table of Numbers
---
