Special Characters

Use ^[HTML Entities] to enter special characters; e.g. write 'd&eacute;tente' as `d\&eacute;tente`. If frequently relying on these characters, it is best to use a keyboard layout for your computer or device that lets you type them directly.

DEMO ---
| &alpha; | &Gamma; | &aacute;
    | &aelig; | &ccedil; | &egrave;
    | &iuml; | &ntilde; | &ocirc;
| &cent; | &pound; | &euro;
    | &deg; | &plusmn; | &frac12;
    | &le; | &ge; | &ne;
| &copy; | &reg; | &trade;
    | &sect; | &para; | &dagger;
    | &laquo; | &raquo; | &middot;
---
